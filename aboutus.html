Our history

Attex Steel fabricators was established in 2009, as a mechanical engineering firm, heavy Steel Fabrication Firm & General building construction Company and has become a reputable player in transformation of the Kenyan fabrication industry professionally. Over the years we have completed numerous Commercial & Industrial Projects of National Importance.

Our executive team is led by the founder Stephen Mwangi Gikunya whose wealth of experience in the Mechanical and structural Engineering, Heavy Fabrication and related Construction fields in diverse industry sectors has driven the company's exponential growth to its currernt successful levels

Our Goal

Our expectation and drive for excellence touches every step in our fabrication system ensuring complex project needs are met with thoughtful precision. In order to make this happen, our teams receive ongoing training allowing for top-level craftsmanship in the shop and safe and efficient steel erection on site.

Security Matters

With over 15 years of solid growth and success behind us, Attex Steel Fabricators is a secure choice on any size project. Attex's financial strength and industry leading bonding capacity ensures the ability to complete projects of all sizes.
